Tutorial

Welcome to the tutorial!

This website has 3D models, so you will need to learn how to use them.

You can practice on this donut.

To start interacting with the model, move your mouse on top of the model.

Click and drag with the left mouse button to rotate the model.

Click and drag with the right mouse button to move the model.

You can also use the arrow keys to move the model.

Scroll up and down to zoom in and out.
