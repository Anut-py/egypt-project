Welcome to the structures of Ancient Egypt!

In this website, you will learn about the Egyptian pyramids by looking at 3D models.

On the top of the screen, you will see a purple bar.

You can click on the links on the bar to go to different pages.

The Home link will take you back here.

The How-To link will show you how to view 3D models.

The Structures link will teach you about Ancient Egypt and show you 3D models.

The Gallery link will showcase just the 3D models.
